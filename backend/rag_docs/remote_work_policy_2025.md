# Remote Work Policy – January 2025 Update

This policy supersedes the March 2022 Remote Work Guidelines effective immediately.

## Updated Remote Work Schedule

| Employee Tier | Remote Days Per Week | Equipment Stipend | Approval Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Junior (0-2 yrs) | 3 days | $500/year | Manager |
| Senior (2-5 yrs) | 4 days | $1,000/year | Self-Service |
| Principal (5+ yrs) | Fully Remote | $1,500/year | None |

## Key Changes from 2022 Policy
- Remote days increased from 2 to 3-5 depending on seniority
- Equipment stipend now provided (previously $0)
- Core hours changed from 9-5 to flexible 10 AM – 4 PM overlap window
- VPN requirement replaced with Zero Trust architecture
