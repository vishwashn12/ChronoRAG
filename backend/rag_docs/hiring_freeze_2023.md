# Company-Wide Hiring Freeze – Q3 2023

Effective July 1, 2023, the company has implemented a company-wide hiring freeze across all departments. No new requisitions will be approved until further notice. All open positions that have not received signed offer letters are to be placed on hold.

Internal transfers remain permitted with VP-level approval. Contract and temporary staff renewals are frozen as well.

This measure is expected to remain in place through Q4 2023 pending economic review.
