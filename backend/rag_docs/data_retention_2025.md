# Data Retention & Privacy Policy – May 2025 (GDPR-Aligned)

This policy supersedes the June 2021 Data Retention Policy in its entirety.

## Retention Schedule

| Data Category | Retention Period | Storage | Encryption | Deletion Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Customer PII | 3 years max | Cloud (encrypted) | AES-256 mandatory | Automated purge |
| Financial Records | 7 years | Hybrid | AES-256 mandatory | Manual review |
| Employee Records | Duration + 2 years | Cloud (encrypted) | AES-256 mandatory | Automated purge |
| Marketing Analytics | 1 year | Cloud | Optional | Automated purge |

## Key Changes from 2021 Policy
- PII retention reduced from indefinite to 3-year maximum
- Cloud storage now permitted (previously prohibited)
- Encryption changed from optional to mandatory (AES-256)
- Data access requests must be fulfilled within 30 days (down from 45)
- On-premises-only requirement removed
