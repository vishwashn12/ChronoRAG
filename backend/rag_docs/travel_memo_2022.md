# Travel and Expense Guidelines – May 2022

All business travel must be approved by the department head at least two weeks in advance. The daily meal allowance is capped at $50 per day regardless of destination. Lodging reimbursement is limited to $200 per night.

All flights must be booked in Economy class. No exceptions for flight duration. Ride-sharing expenses are capped at $40 per day.

Receipts are required for all individual expenses exceeding $15. Client entertainment expenses are not permitted without CEO approval.
