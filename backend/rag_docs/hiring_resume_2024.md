# Hiring Freeze Lifted – February 2024

Effective February 1, 2024, the company-wide hiring freeze initiated in July 2023 has been officially lifted. All departments may resume normal recruitment activity.

New requisitions require Director-level approval. A revised headcount plan has been distributed to all department heads. Priority hiring areas include Engineering, Data Science, and Compliance.

Contract and temporary staff renewals are also reinstated effective immediately.
