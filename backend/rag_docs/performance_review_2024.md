# Annual Performance Review Process – 2024

All employees are subject to annual performance reviews conducted in December of each year. The review cycle consists of a self-assessment, a manager assessment, and a calibration session at the department level.

Performance ratings use a 5-point scale: Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Developing, Below Expectations, and Unsatisfactory.

Employees rated "Below Expectations" for two consecutive years are placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) lasting 90 days. Promotions require a minimum rating of "Meets Expectations" for two consecutive review cycles.

Bonus payouts are directly tied to the performance rating and are distributed in February of the following year.
