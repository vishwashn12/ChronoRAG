# Compensation Framework – 2026 Revision

This document supersedes the April 2023 compensation review.

## Engineering Salary Bands (Effective January 15, 2026)

| Level | Title | Base Salary Range | Annual Bonus | RSU Grant |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| L3 | Entry Engineer | $105,000 – $120,000 | 12% | $20,000/yr |
| L4 | Mid Engineer | $130,000 – $155,000 | 15% | $40,000/yr |
| L5 | Senior Engineer | $160,000 – $200,000 | 20% | $75,000/yr |
| L6 | Staff Engineer | $200,000 – $250,000 | 25% | $120,000/yr |

## Key Changes
- Entry-level base increased from $85,000 to $105,000 minimum
- Bonus targets increased across all levels
- RSU grants now included (previously stock options only)
- 4-year vesting changed to 3-year monthly vesting
