# Data Retention & Privacy Policy – June 2021

The company retains all customer data for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with SOX requirements. Personal data may be stored indefinitely unless a customer requests deletion under CCPA.

Backup archives are maintained on-premises in the company's primary data center. No cloud storage is permitted for customer PII. Encryption at rest is recommended but not mandatory.

Data access requests must be fulfilled within 45 business days. The Data Protection Officer (DPO) is responsible for all compliance escalations.
