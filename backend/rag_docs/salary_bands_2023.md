# Annual Compensation Review – 2023

As of April 2023, the company has standardized salary bands across all departments. The base salary for entry-level software engineers is $85,000 per year. Mid-level engineers earn between $110,000 and $130,000. Senior engineers are compensated in the range of $140,000 to $170,000.

The annual bonus target is 10% of base salary for all engineering roles. Stock options vest over a 4-year cliff schedule with a 1-year cliff.

Cost of living adjustments (COLA) are applied annually in January based on CPI data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics.
