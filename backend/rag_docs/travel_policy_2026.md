# Corporate Travel Policy & Expense Schedule 2026

## 1. Overview
Effective July 23, 2026, the company has updated its global travel and expense guidelines to reflect current economic conditions. This document serves as the active baseline for all corporate travel, client entertainment, and daily allowances. 

**Note to all staff:** This schedule explicitly supersedes all previous travel memorandums, including the 2022 travel expense guidelines.

## 2. Updated Expense Allowance Table
To simplify expense reporting, we have moved from flat unstructured allowances to the following tier-based schedule. 

| Expense Category | Daily Limit (USD) | Receipt Required? | Policy Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Lodging (Standard) | $250.00 | Yes | Active |
| Lodging (Major City) | $350.00 | Yes | Active |
| **Food & Meals** | **$100.00** | **Over $25 only** | **Active (Overrides 2022 $50 cap)** |
| Local Rideshare | $60.00 | Yes | Active |
| Client Entertainment | $150.00 | Yes (Requires VP approval) | Active |

## 3. Booking Procedures
All flights and lodging must be booked through the approved corporate travel portal (Navan/Concur). Flights under 6 hours are strictly limited to Economy class. Flights over 6 hours are eligible for Premium Economy or Business Class with prior departmental approval.

## 4. Enforcement
Any expenses exceeding the tabular limits above will require out-of-pocket payment by the employee unless an emergency waiver is granted by the Finance team prior to the transaction.
