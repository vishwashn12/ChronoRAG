# Remote Work Guidelines – March 2022

Effective March 15, 2022, the company permits all full-time employees to work remotely up to 2 days per week. Employees must be physically present at the office for at least 3 days per week.

Remote work requests must be approved by the direct manager at least one week in advance. No equipment stipend is provided for home offices.

Employees working remotely are expected to be available during core hours (9 AM – 5 PM local time). VPN access is mandatory for all remote sessions.
